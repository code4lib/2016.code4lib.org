Diversity Scholarships Angel Fund

As part of the Code4Lib community's ongoing committment to helping under-represented groups excel in computing and technology, we offer multiple scholarships to attend the annual conference. In addition to seeking organization sponsors for scholarships, we are now able to accept donations from individuals who would like to support the scholarship program. Indiviual donors wishing to be named will be thanked on the Sponsors page, though anonyuous donation is also possible.

Donate Now

All funds raised will help pay for transportation, accommodation, and conference registration costs for Code4Lib's Diversity Scholarships recipients for our annual conference. Please feel free to direct any question to the Scholarship Committee via chadbnelson at gmail dot com.
